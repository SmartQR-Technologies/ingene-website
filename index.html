About Us

At InGene, we are on a mission to bridge healthcare gaps with affordable, high-precision solutions that deliver fast, actionable insights. Our innovative point-of-care technologies make it easier to track and monitor essential health parameters.

Powered by AI and machine learning, our systems reduce the need for bulky equipment, minimize human intervention, and enhance accuracy. By putting real-time health data in people’s hands, we enable informed decisions and promote a proactive approach to well-being—accessible to all, regardless of location or socio-economic background.
