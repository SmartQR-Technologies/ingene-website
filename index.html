About Us

At INGENE, we are redefining the future of genomics and molecular diagnostics. Our mission is to make advanced DNA analysis more accessible, accurate, and efficient through a unique blend of science and technology. By combining deep expertise in biotechnology with next-generation AI and ML capabilities, we empower healthcare providers and researchers with solutions that are both innovative and practical.

From preventive healthcare to infectious disease monitoring and pharmacogenetics, our platform is designed to bridge the gap between innovation and real-world application. With a relentless focus on precision, scalability, and ease of use, INGENE stands as a trusted partner in the journey toward smarter, more inclusive healthcare for all.

Preventative

Preventative

Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM) is a silent threat often left undetected. INGENE identifies risk markers like MYBPC3 to enable early intervention, reducing sudden cardiac arrest risk by up to 80%. Our platform helps personalize treatment plans using pharmacogenomic data.

Pharmacogenetics

Pharmacogenetics

From breast and lung to ovarian and prostate cancer, INGENE uses FDA-approved biomarkers to identify mutations like CYP2C8, ABCB1, and BRAF. This allows clinicians to detect cancers earlier and personalize chemotherapy effectiveness and drug choice.
